SIFT Launches EHS Platform to Make Manufacturers Safer and Smarter

Company News August 28, 2025

SIFT announced the launch of its groundbreaking technology designed to help manufacturers proactively manage workplace safety, regulatory compliance, and operational risk.

SIFT announced the launch of its groundbreaking technology designed to help manufacturers proactively manage workplace safety, regulatory compliance, and operational risk. Built for high-hazard industries, SIFT uses conversational AI and regulatory intelligence to provide real-time compliance insights, automate safety documentation, and streamline incident tracking.

"Manufacturers face rising pressure from regulators, labor shortages, and the need for safer, smarter operations. SIFT exists to close that gap, giving factories an affordable, scalable way to stay compliant while protecting their people and processes."
— Aryaman Thareja, CEO of SIFT

The platform's core capabilities include an AI-powered regulation engine that provides instant access to verified OSHA/EPA requirements with contextual guidance, automated Job Hazard Analysis (JHA) and SOP generation with customizable, exportable reports, and a comprehensive training and incident tracking dashboard that manages OSHA 10/30 completion, incident logs, corrective actions, and OSHA 300 compliance.

Looking ahead, the company plans to expand its ecosystem by partnering with OSHA-authorized trainers, ergonomics experts, and industry associations to deliver a "full-stack AI EHS consulting" model that integrates both technology and human expertise. This comprehensive approach will provide manufacturers with complete EHS support systems that combine cutting-edge AI technology with proven human expertise and industry best practices.
