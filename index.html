Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about AI-powered compliance automation

How does SIFT ensure accuracy without hallucinations or bias?

SIFT uses verified regulatory databases and context-specific AI agents that are trained on accurate, up-to-date compliance data. Our system includes multiple verification layers and real-time cross-referencing to eliminate hallucinations and ensure accurate compliance guidance.

Can SIFT integrate with our existing manufacturing systems?

Yes, SIFT is designed for seamless integration with existing manufacturing systems, IoT devices, cameras, sensors, and ERP platforms. Our implementation team works with you to ensure minimal disruption to current operations.

What types of compliance regulations does SIFT support?

SIFT supports OSHA safety regulations, EPA environmental compliance, ISO standards, FDA requirements, and custom industry-specific regulations. Our system continuously updates with new regulatory changes and your uploaded documents.

Is SIFT suitable for small to medium-sized manufacturers?

Absolutely. SIFT scales from small facilities to large enterprise operations. Our flexible pricing and modular approach make advanced compliance automation accessible for manufacturers of all sizes.
