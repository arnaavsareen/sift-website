Rethinking the Way EHS Works

Making compliance as simple as having a conversation

Our Mission

SIFT is rethinking the way EHS works. We believe that language is far more natural, intuitive, and accessible than clunky legacy software. And that everything from complex regulations to daily checklists should be as simple as having a conversation.

By speaking your language, SIFT removes barriers, streamlines workflows, and puts control back in your team's hands. No more hunting through nested menus or toggling between half a dozen apps: just ask SIFT for a dynamic job hazard analysis, request an SOP for your new work cell, or check the status of training completions and get instant, actionable guidance.

Built by Engineers, Designed for Manufacturers

Two Cornell engineers with a vision to make factories safer and smarter

SIFT combines a compliance LLM trained on OSHA, EPA, FDA, and beyond with real-time monitoring and automated reporting features. Whether you're tracking fall-protection protocols, scheduling respirator fit tests, or generating JHAs on the fly, our conversational interface puts you in command, without the learning curve.
