Our Mission

EHS shouldn't require a manual.

At SIFT, we're transforming environmental, health, and safety management from a bureaucratic burden into a simple conversation. While legacy systems force you to navigate endless menus and juggle multiple apps, SIFT understands plain English and delivers exactly what you need, instantly.

Need a dynamic job hazard analysis? Just ask. Want to check training compliance across your facility? One question gets you there. From generating SOPs for new equipment to scheduling respirator fit tests, SIFT handles complex EHS tasks through natural dialogue.
